🚗 How to Reach

  • From Dehradun: Take the Mussoorie Road and follow the route towards Woodstock School. The property is located near Village Masrana, approximately 45-60 minutes drive.
  • From Mussoorie: Drive towards Woodstock School area and follow the signs to Village Masrana. The journey takes about 45-60 minutes.
  • By Train: Dehradun Railway Station is the nearest railhead (approximately 25 km). You can take a taxi from there to reach the property.
  • By Air: Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun is the nearest airport (approximately 35 km). Taxis are readily available from the airport.
